Marriage Survey • 25+ Years Married

Marriage Survey – 2026

I am interviewing people who have been married for 25 years or more to understand what helps a marriage last over time.

Your answers are confidential and will only be used for a school project.

Basic Information

Please complete all items. You must have been married 25 years or more.

Survey Questions

Short paragraphs are perfect. Feel free to use your own words and examples.

1. How long have you been married, and how old were you and your spouse when you got married? *

2. How, when, and where did you and your spouse first meet, and what first attracted you to each other? *

3. How old were you and your spouse when you each first started seriously dating, and how long had you known each other before you began dating? *

4. Do you consider your marriage successful? Why or why not? Please give the top two or three reasons you feel positive about your marriage. *

5. Do you think your age difference or closeness in age has affected your success in marriage? Please explain. *

6. How do you and your spouse usually handle disagreements or arguments, and what compromises have you had to make over the years? *

7. How are financial problems or money decisions handled in your marriage, and what advice about money would you give younger couples? *

8. What things do you and your spouse enjoy doing together now, and how do you keep closeness or romance alive after many years? *

9. Looking back, what has surprised you most about marriage compared to what you expected before you got married, and is there anything you would do differently? *

10. What advice would you give a young woman and a young man today who want a strong, long‑lasting marriage? *
